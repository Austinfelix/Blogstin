Chocolate Brownie

An easy to bake and easier to eat dish that always tastes better
the more you bake it at home, this dish dose'nt require speacial
ingredients or equipments to prepare as everything yo do need is
easily accessible at your nearby stores.
A WORD OF CAUTION: Keep in mind the right temperature and
timing to bake the brownie in the oven.
So let's begin.

Ingredients

Sr.No. Name Measurement
1 Chocolate 350 gm
2 Butter 250 gm
3 Sugar 375 gm
4 Egg 3 nos.
5 Flour 375 gm
6 Vanilla Essence 1-2 drops
7 Salt 1 pinch
8 Cocoa powder 150 gm

Temperature's

Reason Temperature time
Pre-heating 150oC 5 minutes
Baking 180oC 25-30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate along with butter using double boiler method.
  2. Whisk together the egg & sugar, whisk to attain ribbon-like consistency.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and 2 drops of vanilla essence into the egg.
  4. Add flour into the egg mixture & mix well. (AVOID LUMP FORMATION)
  5. Add in the chocolate and butter mixture along with cocoa powder into the egg mix.
  6. It is optional to add Corn-flour, as it gives the brownie a crunchy exterior and goeey inside.
  7. If you want you could add nuts and raisins into your brownie to enhance taste.
  8. It is also optional to add Baking Powder, to give the brownie a cake like texture.
  9. Now is the right time to pre-heat the oven at 150oC for 5 minutes.
  10. Take a cake mould and grease well and cover with butter paper(DO NOT REQUIRED FOR NON-STICK MOULDS)
  11. Pour in the batter onto the mould and put into the oven at 180oC for 25-30 minutes.
  12. Dip in a tooth-pick to see if the cake is done, as it would come out clean.
  13. Now Enjoy your Chocolate Brownie!
