Idli Chilli

With the constant innovation of several dishes across diferent
cuisines, here is Idli Chilli which is a Indo-Chinese dish classified
under street food in several parts of India.
Its Deep fried Idli tossed around with sauces like soy sauce,vinegar
and schezwan along with few vgetables.
It is a very tasty dish to have as a snack along with your tea or coffee,
So lets begin cooking.

Ingredients:-

Sr.No. Name Measurement
1 Idli 5 nos.
2 onion 1 medium sized
3 Capsicum 1/4 nos.
4 Corn flour/flour 2 tbsp.
5 Red Chilli Powder 1/2 tsp.
6 Soy Sauce 0.5 tbsp.
7 Schezwan Sauce 1 tbsp.
8 Vinegar(synthetic) 0.5 tbsp.
9 Garlic 4 cloves
10 Salt As reqd.

Instructions:-

  1. Cut the idli's into irregular shapes.
  2. Mix together the idli, red chilli powder, 1 tsp.soysauce & cornflour.
  3. Make sure the idli is evenly coated by the flour mixture.
  4. Deep fry the idli's and make sure the the idli comes out golden in colour.
  5. Cut the Onion and Capsicum into regular shapes also chop some garlic.
  6. Stif fry the chopped vegetables along with the garlic.
  7. Add in soysauce, vinegar and some schezwan along with the vegetables.
  8. Add in water and mix well
  9. IMPORTANT STEP:-We must add in a corn-flour slurry to thicken the gravy.
    10. Corn-flour slurry is prepared by mixing cornflour with water.
  10. Mix the gravy nicelly and once it is thick enough, add in the idli.
  11. check for salt and add some
