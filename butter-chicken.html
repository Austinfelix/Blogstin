Butter Chicken

The most famous dish all over the world for all Indians is butter chicken,
apart from being everyones usual at the family diner it is also very delicious
and sort of easy to make if you follow the right steps!
Butter chicken originated from the streets of New Delhi,India.
Many chefs all over the world experiment with this dish and provide
their speacialty to it for their restaurants.
So lets see how to prepare this great dish!

Ingredients:-

Sr. No. Name Measurement
1 Cardamom 2-3 nos.
2 Cinnamon 2-3 nos.
3 Clove 2-3 nos.
4 Bay Leaf 2 nos.
5 Garlic 4 nos.
6 Cashew Nut As req.
7 Cooking Oil 3-4 tbsp.
8 Onion(chopped) 1.5 nos.
9 Tomatoes(blanched) 2 nos.
10 Water 150 ml
11 Fresh Cream 1.5 cup
12 Butter As reqd.
13 Chicken(boneless) 500-750gm
14 Red Chilli Powder 2.5 tsp.
15 Salt 2.5 tsp.
16 Turmeric Powder 1 tsp.
17 Garam Masala 1 tsp.
18 Coriander Powder 1 tsp.
19 Fenugreek leaves As reqd.
20 Coriander As reqd.
21 Hung Curd 5 tbsp.
22 Pepper 1 tsp.

Instruction:-

  1. Marinate the chicken along with curd,pepper,salt & red chilli powder
  2. Keep aside the marnated chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Blanch the tomatoes and peel the skin away and chop the tomatoes
    4. Blanching is the technique of cooking vegetables in boiling water until they become soft.
  4. Take a pan with oil and over medium flame add Cinnamon,cardamom,clove,bayleaf & garlic.
  5. After some team add in the Onion, make sure the garlic isnt burnt.
  6. After the onion turns golden brown add in the chopped tomatoes
  7. Add in the necessary masalas i.e. red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander masala etc.
  8. Mix the tomato and onion mixture well and let cool.
  9. In a separate pan stir fry the marinated chicken and set aside.(do not overcook the meat)
  10. Grind roasted cashews in a food processor and add little water to make into fine paste.
  11. Now we must process the onion and tomatoe mixture in the food processor to get a fine gravy.
  12. In a pan add in the processed gravy with water over a medium flame
  13. Add in the rest of the masalas i.e. garam masala & aamchur(optional)etc.
  14. Add the cashew paste now and also mix in the fenugreek leaves.
  15. Now add in your Favourite amount of butter, check and respectively add salt
  16. Now in goes the chicken and leave the gravy to simmer.
  17. if you like your butter chicken sweet then add honey and mix well
  18. For garnish add coriander and some fresh cream.
  19. Serve alongside with naan.
